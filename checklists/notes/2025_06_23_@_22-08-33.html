🔍 What it’s about:

BIM Coordination ensures that:


  • All project elements fit together spatially (no clashes or overlaps).
  • Design information is consistent and up-to-date across all teams.
  • Construction teams can build accurately from the model without surprises.

🛠️ Typical BIM Coordination Activities:

  • Clash detection (e.g., identifying where ducts intersect beams).
  • Model federation (combining models from different disciplines into one).
  • Design reviews using platforms like Navisworks, BIM 360, or Solibri.
  • Coordination meetings based on findings in the model.
  • Issue tracking and resolution through tools like BCF or BIM Track.
