"Identifies and engages all project stakeholders."

This involves systematically determining all individuals, groups, or organizations that are impacted by or can influence the project. It includes:


  • Identification – Listing internal and external stakeholders (e.g. client, users, contractors, regulators).
  • Analysis – Understanding their needs, influence, and expectations.
  • Engagement Planning – Defining how and when to involve each stakeholder.
  • Communication – Keeping stakeholders informed, consulted, or involved as appropriate.
  • Monitoring – Tracking changes in stakeholder positions and adjusting strategies accordingly.

Goal: Build support, reduce resistance, and ensure alignment throughout the project lifecycle.
