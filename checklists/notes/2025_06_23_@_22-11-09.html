What it is:

A federated 3D model that combines models from all disciplines (architecture, structure, MEP, etc.).

Purpose:


  • Used for clash detection, spatial checks, and coordination reviews.
  • Allows different teams to see how their work fits with others.
  • Often used in tools like Navisworks or BIM Collaborate Pro.

Key features:


  • Not used for drawings directly.
  • Focused on geometry, interference, and data sharing.
  • Updated regularly with new versions from design teams
